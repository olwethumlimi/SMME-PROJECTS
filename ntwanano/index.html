About Us

Ntwanano Solutions is a 100% black owned company.

The vision of the company is to provide services which benefit the targeted Clients.

These services are secure, flexible and priceable.

That is one of the key factors that differentiate Ntwanano Solutions services and that of other price driven companies.

Flexibility and the priority to satisfy the needs of our Clients in time make Ntwanano Solutions a company to partner with in serving your needs.

Our priority is that one of serving our Clients with loyalty and diligence as they are valuable to us.

We strive making all products needed by our Clients to be cost effective, with the mandate given to us by our Clients.

We strive to do what is best wanted by our Clients according to the specifications, which will allow for us to get the best in the market for our respectable Clients.

We are not all about selling of services and products, but we are all about assisting our Clients in attaining the overall goals and desired objectives.

We specifically look at satisfying the specific needs of our Clients and by understanding their priorities enables us to develop flexible solutions for future growth.
