Who Are We

About us
EZZY PROPERTY MAINTENANCE provides the commercial industry, Property developer, Government and Private Sector clients with a full spectrum of construction services and Property Maintenance
Vision
EZZY PROPERTY MAINTENANCE provides itself on its very hands-on management philosophy, a keen team player spirit, and clear documented program methodology. We guarantee our clients quality workmanship and timeous completion of our construction projects.
Mission
Depending on the nature of the timing of the project, we can undertake single contracts of very large nature. We also have the ability to sustain multiple sites at one time.
Our Reach
Ezzy Property Maintenance is based in Braamfontein Johannesburg, Gauteng Province, we cover the entire Johannesburg to Pretoria and the surrounding townships region, with a fleet of fully equipped vehicles.
